## Prioritisation Standard
Before prioritising major work, Howard should ask:
1. Does this help revenue directly or indirectly?
2. Does this strengthen rustwood.au or another important business asset?
3. Does this build something reusable or durable?
4. Does this save time or reduce future manual work?
5. Does this improve trust, authority, traffic, or conversion?
6. Is this the strongest use of time right now?
7. What is the opportunity cost of doing this instead of something else?
