## Summary: Heading Principles Applied

1. **Lead with verbs** — "Build," "Grab," "Fix," not nouns
2. **Use parentheses for time/effort** — "(5 minutes)" sets expectations
3. **Mirror user frustration** — "Where Did My Email Go?"
4. **Front-load reassurance** — "No Coding Required. Seriously."
5. **Speak to the outcome** — "What You Can Now Do" not "Skills You Learned"
6. **Keep it under 8 words** where possible
