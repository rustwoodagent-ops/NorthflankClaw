# Social Proof & Testimonial Collection Strategy
## For Rustwood Operator OS

---

## COLLECTION METHODS

### 1. Early Access Beta Users
**Target:** First 10-20 early access subscribers who engage
**Method:** Direct email request after 7 days of usage
**Incentive:** Extended access, future product discounts, featured mention

### 2. Operator OS Community (Future)
**Target:** Active community members
**Method:** In-app/request prompts at milestone moments
**Timing:** After successful workflow implementation

### 3. Case Study Partnerships
**Target:** Founders with measurable results
**Method:** Direct outreach for detailed case study
**Benefit:** Co-marketing opportunity, extended support

---

## TESTIMONIAL FORMATS

### Short Quotes
- 1-2 sentences
- Specific outcome or benefit
- Name + role + (optional) company

### Medium Testimonials  
- 3-5 sentences
- Before/after context
- Specific feature or framework that helped
- Name + role + company

### Case Studies
- 500-1000 words
- Full transformation story
- Metrics where possible (time saved, revenue impact)
- Interview format with quotes

---

## QUESTIONS TO ASK

1. What was your biggest operational challenge before using Operator OS?
2. Which specific framework or template helped you most?
3. What concrete outcome or improvement have you seen?
4. How would you describe Operator OS to another founder?
5. What surprised you most about using the system?

---

## USAGE GUIDELINES

**Always obtain:**
- Explicit permission to use quote
- Preferred attribution (name/role/company)
- Photo (optional but preferred)

**Never:**
- Fabricate or edit testimonials misleadingly
- Use without permission
- Attribute incorrectly

---

## PLACEMENT STRATEGY

### Product Page
- 3 short testimonials in grid below features
- Rotating carousel (future enhancement)

### Sales/Checkout Page
- 1 strong medium testimonial
- Quote highlights trust/clarity

### Email Nurture
- 1 testimonial per email (rotating)
- Contextually relevant to email content

### Authority Content
- Inline testimonials in articles
- Links to full case studies
