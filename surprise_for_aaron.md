**Fun Fact for the Morning:**

Did you know that the first computer bug was an actual moth? On September 9, 1947, Grace Hopper found a moth trapped in a relay of the Harvard Mark II computer. She taped it into the logbook with the note: "First actual case of bug being found." The term "debugging" had been used before, but this was the first literal computer bug.
